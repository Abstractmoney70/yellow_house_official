"Be the change you wish to see in the world." — Mahatma Gandhi

Gandhi House is one of VWMS's founding houses, named for one of history's great figures; a man who proved that quiet conviction can move mountains. We carry that name seriously.

We compete hard in every event, but what sets us apart is what happens off the scoreboard: the stories written at midnight, the songs composed for Sports Day, the ARGs that keep classmates up solving ciphers. We are athletes, writers, musicians, and mystery-makers.

This site is our home. It will grow as we grow.
