"Be the change you wish to see in the world." — Mahatma Gandhi

Gandhi House is one of VWMS's founding houses, named for a man who proved that quiet conviction can move mountains. We take that name seriously; it means something to be Gandhi.

We compete hard in every event, sure. But what really sets us apart happens off the scoreboard: stories written at midnight, songs composed for Sports Day, ARGs that keep classmates up solving ciphers till 2am. We're athletes and writers; musicians and mystery-makers; competitors and creators.

This site is our home. It'll grow as we grow.
